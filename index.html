About the Band

Shattered Riffs is a high-energy rock band known for powerful live performances and raw sound.

Our Influences

Classic Rock

The classic rock genre encompasses rock music made over three decades, from '60s psychedelia and '70s album-oriented rock (AOR) to 1980s college rock, heavy metal, and 1990's grunge.

Alternative

Alternative music is music that hasn't yet achieved a mainstream audience ... Alternative music is any kind of music that has the potential to reach a wider audience..

Hard Rock

Hard rock is a form of loud, aggressive rock music. The electric guitar is often emphasised, used with distortion and other effects, both as a rhythm instrument using repetitive riffs with a varying degree of complexity, and as a solo lead instrument..
